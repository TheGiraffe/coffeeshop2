About Personal Peace Cafe

This cafe is imagined as a place where you can reconnect with yourself. It is welcome to people of all faiths, cultures, and backgrounds. For Korean-American Buddhists raised in the West, this space may also provide a familiarity and help you feel connected to Korean culture. For everyone else, in addition to the environment of calm and restfulness, we hope you enjoy learning more about Korean culture and Buddhism through exploring this space.
